How are you feeling energy-wise?`;

I'd recommend getting pandas to at least 60% before diving into portfolio projects.\n\nThis isn't about perfection - it's about having enough foundation to create quality work without getting frustrated.       return `I hear you. Career transitions are marathons, not sprints, and it's completely normal to have challenging days.\n\n✨ **Remember**: You've maintained a ${stats.streak}-day streak and earned ${stats.totalPoints} points.
